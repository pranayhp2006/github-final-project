Our Pledge

We, as members and leaders of the ABC Tech Community, pledge to make participation in our community a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone. We value diversity and are committed to creating an environment where all members feel welcome and supported.

Our Standards
Expected Behavior
Be respectful and professional in all interactions.
Listen to others and consider different viewpoints.
Offer constructive feedback and assistance.
Support a collaborative and positive community culture.
Unacceptable Behavior
Harassment, discrimination, or bullying.
Offensive comments related to personal characteristics.
Threats, intimidation, or abusive language.
Sharing another person's private information without permission.
Enforcement Responsibilities

Community moderators and administrators are responsible for:

Monitoring community discussions and activities.
Responding to reports of misconduct.
Applying this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently.
Maintaining confidentiality whenever possible.
Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

Community forums and discussion boards.
Social media channels managed by the community.
Online meetings, webinars, and workshops.
In-person events organized by the ABC Tech Community.
Enforcement

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the moderation team at:

Email: conduct@abctech.org

Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially. Appropriate action will be taken based on the nature and severity of the violation.

Enforcement Guidelines
Level 1: Correction

For minor violations, a moderator may privately explain why the behavior was inappropriate and request that it stop.

Level 2: Warning

For repeated or moderate violations, a formal warning will be issued and documented.

Level 3: Temporary Suspension

For serious misconduct, the individual may be suspended from participating in community activities for a specified period.

Level 4: Permanent Ban

For severe harassment, threats, discrimination, or repeated violations, the individual may be permanently removed from the community.

Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the principles of the Contributor Covenant and adapted to meet the needs of the ABC Tech Community.
